About Our Company

Built On Decades Of Trust & Expertise

Founded in 1995 by Mr. Mangesh Kulkarni, M.M. Enterprises has grown into one of Pune's most respected names in waterproofing, structural repair, and construction chemical supply.

We work alongside the city's most prominent structural consultants and builders, delivering reliable solutions that protect and strengthen the structures people depend on every day.

As an Authorised Stockist for leading global brands, we bring certified, high-performance products to every project — backed by hands-on execution and decades of field expertise.

What We Do

Our Core Services

⚒

Structural Repair

Restoration and strengthening of beams, columns, slabs and foundations using polymer-modified repair systems.

🔩

Anchoring

Chemical and mechanical anchoring for critical load-bearing connections in concrete, masonry and steel substrates.

🛡

Waterproofing

Membrane, cementitious and crystalline waterproofing for terraces, basements and external facades.

🏭

Floor Coatings

Epoxy, polyurethane and cementitious floor systems for factories, warehouses, clean rooms and commercial spaces.

⚙

Steel Fabrication

Custom trusses, portal frames, mezzanines and industrial sheds — designed, fabricated and erected on site.
